Registrations are live.

An annual Hackathon to compete in!

The Event & Task

Running from 9th of June through to the 23rd of June. You can work alone, or in a team of up to 3 people! With your project being pitted against other teams with a winner being crowned at the end. 🏆

The brief is basic and simple. Build a single level of a Game, that must have a defined start and end. 🎮 You can build it using whatever you wish but it must be in a playable state for us to judge. Want to use Unity? Or PhaserJS and make it a browser game? Go for it! Literally there is no limitation on how you build it.

For the winners, we have secured Lifetime access to Bruno Simon's ThreeJS Journey Course! This course teaches 3D in the browser through WebGL and covers WebGL & ThreeJS topics such as Physics, Renderers, Blender, React Three Fiber, and so much more! Take a look at Bruno's course and portfolio site to learn more!
